| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-06-COLOR | (accessibility) | colour-only signal fails WCAG 1.4.1 | mitigate | every amber/coral state carries a worded text label/badge (gauge figure + label; recon/stuck badges) |
